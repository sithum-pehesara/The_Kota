Who is The Kota?

Madhusha Nethmal, better known as "The Kota," is one of Sri Lanka's most popular YouTubers and content creators.

He blew up thanks to his hilarious reaction videos, where he dives into viral memes, internet culture, and random videos with his signature sense of humor.

Today, his loyal community, the ChootyGang, is hundreds of thousands strong across all platforms.
